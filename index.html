Who we are

Nexture is a new-age, technology-first service platform revolutionizing the way urban households access everyday services. We connect customers to a curated network of trained and background-verified professionals across categories such as home cleaning, beauty at home, appliance repair, plumbing, electrical work, and more — all available at the tap of a button or a simple WhatsApp message.

Our mission is simple: to make professional-grade services as seamless, affordable, and trustworthy — while empowering service providers with fair earnings, performance-based growth, and continuous training.

We’re not just building a service marketplace — we’re building the future lifestyle infrastructure of every modern home.
